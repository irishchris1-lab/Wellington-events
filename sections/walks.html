🦜 Wildlife Walk
Kāpiti Island Nature Reserve
⏱ 3–4 hr · Moderate (steep sections)
Rare native birds Predator-free island Ferry from Paraparaumu
One of NZ's great wildlife experiences — a predator-free island sanctuary where kiwi roam freely during the day, giant weta hide in the trees and kōkako fill the air. The summit track is steep but worth every step. Booking essential; numbers strictly limited. Best for kids 8+.
