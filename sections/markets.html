Wellington City
🌊 Waterfront Market
Harbourside Market
Every Sunday 25,000+ visitors 30+ food trucks Free entry
Wellington's most beloved Sunday tradition — up to 25,000 visitors each week browsing fresh fruit and veg, 30+ food trucks, live music and local artisan stalls on the beautiful waterfront beside Te Papa. Arrive early for the best produce and a peaceful wander before the crowds build. An essential Wellington experience for the whole family.

🛍 Indoor Market
Wellington Underground Market
Every Saturday Local makers Street food Live music
Wellington's beloved weekly market returns every Saturday — locally made gifts, handcrafted jewellery, vintage finds, street food and live music all under one roof. Usually held at Frank Kitts Underground Carpark (10am–4pm); occasionally moves to San Fran on Cuba St. Free to browse — budget for the excellent food stalls. A great rainy-day outing the whole family can enjoy.

🥦 Farmers Market
Hill Street Farmers Market
Every Saturday Organic produce Artisan bakers Free entry
Fresh local produce, organic goods, natural skincare, coffee, sourdough and pastries in the carpark of St Paul's Cathedral in Thorndon. A wonderful Saturday morning ritual for families — pick up the weekend's kai and grab a coffee while the kids run around. One of Wellington's most community-spirited markets.

🍳 Food Market
City Market — Atrium on the Waterfront
Every Sunday Artisan producers Chef demos Free entry
Local food and beverage producers, chef demonstrations and a relaxed waterfront atmosphere in the beautiful Chaffers Dock atrium. Pick up artisan cheese, local wine, freshly baked bread and specialty coffee. A more intimate market experience than Harbourside — perfect for foodies who want to meet the makers.

🌿 Farmers Market
Riverbank Market
Every Saturday Fresh produce Local makers Free entry
Lower Hutt's beloved weekly market in the heart of the city's Civic Precinct — an early-morning Saturday ritual with fresh fruit and veg, artisan food stalls, local crafts and a great community atmosphere. Worth the early start for the best pick of the produce. Conveniently located beside the Dowse Art Museum.

Upper Hutt's Sunday market set in the vibrant Brewtown precinct — a community hub of craft breweries, food businesses and makers. Wairarapa
🥕 Farmers Market
Martinborough Village Farmers Market
Every Sunday Local produce Street food Free entry
One of the best Sunday day trips from Wellington — a charming village square filled with local produce, street food stalls and artisan goods, with the renowned Martinborough wine trail right on the doorstep. An hour over the Remutaka Hill and well worth the drive. Combine with a bike ride between cellar doors for the perfect Wairarapa Sunday.
