Zealandia — Te Māra a Tāne
World-first urban sanctuary Free-roaming kiwi Guided night tours Kākā · tuatara

See tuatara up close, spot kākā landing in the trees, and join a twilight kiwi tour for something truly unforgettable. One of the most extraordinary wildlife experiences in New Zealand, right in Wellington's backyard.

Wairarapa
🦤 Wildlife Centre
Pukaha National Wildlife Centre
Manukura — white kiwi Tuatara & kokako Old-growth forest Free-flying native birds
Home of Manukura — NZ's famous white kiwi — and one of the country's most successful wildlife sanctuaries. Walk through genuine old-growth native forest with free-flying kōkako, tuatara in their enclosures and the extraordinary experience of seeing kiwi in the nocturnal house. One of NZ's finest wildlife experiences and well worth the hour's drive to Mount Bruce. Unforgettable for the whole family.
